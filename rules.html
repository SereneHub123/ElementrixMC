Elementrix Rules

These rules ensure a fair, enjoyable, and respectful environment for all players. By joining Elementrix you agree to follow them.

Last updated: December 13, 2025

1. General Conduct

  1. Respect All Players: Treat everyone with courtesy. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, threats, or targeted abuse are prohibited.
  2. No Excessive Toxicity: Light competitive banter is allowed; sustained bullying or provoking is not.
  3. Appropriate Language: Avoid excessive swearing, sexual content, or other inappropriate public discussion. Keep it welcoming.
  4. Staff Respect: Follow staff instructions. Impersonating staff or publicly arguing with staff decisions is not allowed.

2. Chat Rules

  1. No Spamming or Flooding: Repeated messages, emojis, or command spam are not permitted.
  2. No Advertising: Do not advertise other servers, Discords, channels, or services without staff permission.
  3. No Scamming or Lying for Gain: Deceiving players to obtain items, currency, or advantages is prohibited.
  4. Channel Use: Keep discussions relevant to their channel (e.g., trading in trade channels).

3. Gameplay Rules

  1. 3.1 Griefing & Raiding:

    Griefing is permitted on Elementrix. Players are responsible for protecting their own builds. Raiding bases, breaking structures, and taking items is allowed unless explicitly restricted for an event or protected region. Complaints about standard griefing or raiding will generally not lead to rollbacks.

    Exception: Griefing intended to lag the server, target harassment, or abuse game mechanics is prohibited.

  2. 3.2 PvP (Player vs Player):

    PvP is allowed unless you are in a protected or PvP-disabled zone. Spawn-killing or repeatedly targeting the same player to harass them is not allowed.

  3. 3.3 Trades & Scamming:

    Player-to-player trades are at your own risk. Staff generally will not refund items lost to scams unless explicitly stated for rare, exceptional cases.

4. Cheating & Exploits

  1. No Hacked Clients or Unfair Mods: Using X-ray, kill aura, fly hacks, auto-clickers, or similar tools is strictly prohibited.
  2. Bug & Exploit Abuse: Do not exploit bugs or glitches for gain. Report discovered bugs to staff immediately.
  3. Lag & Server Abuse: Creating lag machines, excessive entity spawns, or other behavior intended to degrade server stability is prohibited.

5. Builds & World Rules

  1. Inappropriate Builds: Builds containing offensive symbols, sexual content, or hate imagery are not allowed.
  2. Spawn & Protected Areas: Do not grief, exploit, or bypass protections in spawn or other protected regions.
  3. Real-Life Threats: Any build or message implying real-life violence or threats will result in immediate punishment.

6. Economy & Items

  1. Duplication: Item duplication of any kind is prohibited.
  2. Abusing Economy Systems: Exploiting shops, crates, rewards, or plugins for unfair profit is not allowed.
  3. Custom Items & Abilities: Abusing custom items, perks, or abilities beyond intended use may result in punishment.

7. Alternate Accounts (Alts)

  1. Alt Abuse: Using alternate accounts to bypass punishments, farm rewards, or gain unfair advantages is not allowed.
  2. Account Responsibility: You are responsible for actions taken on your account. Sharing accounts is discouraged.

8. Staff & Punishments

  1. Staff Authority: Staff decisions are final in-game. Context and intent will be considered.
  2. Punishment Escalation: Repeated or severe offenses may lead to escalating punishments.
  3. No Public Complaints: Do not publicly argue punishments. Use the designated appeal channels (Discord/support).

9. Rule Changes

  1. Updates: Rules may be updated at any time. It is the player’s responsibility to stay informed.
  2. Unlisted Situations: If a situation is not covered, staff judgment will be applied.

10. Final Notes

  1. By playing on Elementrix, you agree to these rules. "I didn’t know" is not a valid excuse.
  2. Have fun, play fair, and enjoy the chaos — responsibly.

Questions or appeals: reach out on our Discord or the official support channel.
